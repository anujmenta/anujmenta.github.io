Our technology platform assists retail merchants deliver a modern customer experience, through a convergence of transactional and loyalty interactions, using smartphones and other channels.


Our Value proposition lies in providing end-to-end solution to merchants for on-boarding ( connect ) their customers, analyse patterns in their interactions ( comprehend ), and then engage them through relevant and contextual messaging ( communicate ).

Connect

Incentivise and delight your customers with smartphone app, feature phone, gift cards, prepaid wallet syste. We integrate with your Point-of-Sale, so your customer on-boarding experience is seamelss.

Comprehend

Analyse and attract your customer engagement with our Hosted Analytics Portal. Learn from customer's purchasing profile and identify interesting patterns in repeat customers.

Communicate

Easily segment your customers across different platforms like - purchase amount, visit frequency, affinity for products, store location wise, etc - Identify and target them using our in-built bulk messaging platform
